Research Experiences

Extracting Spatio-temporal Information from Contextual Information

UNT | Feb 2021 - Aug 2021
  • Enhance the corpus of spatiotemporal information extraction.
  • Analyze the annotation changes brought by the context tweets.
  • Build context-aware neural networks to incorporate the contextual information.
  • Experimental results show that the contextual information is useful for determining spatiotemporal information.
  • Error analyses show that besides contextual information, more works need to be done to detect bot and spammer.

Spatio-temporal Knowledge Mining

UNT | Aug 2019 - Feb 2021
  • Download tweets using Tweepy and GetOldTweets3.
  • Design interface to collect annotations.
  • Collect annotations using Amazon Mechanical Turk, identify bad annotators and filter out annotations using "Multi-Annotator Competence Estimation"
  • Single-modal neural networks and multi-modal models are built. Experimental results show that both text and image are useful to determine the location of the authors of the tweets.
  • Error analyses show that advertisement tweets significantly affect the model performance, and contextual information is also essential.

Temporal Ordering of Locations

UNT | Sep 2019 - Apr 2021
  • Determine whether the author of the travelogue is located in the mentioned location or not.
  • Order the visited location in every document of the travelogue.
  • Error analysis shows that contextual information is essential to order the visited locations.

Natural Language Identification

Pitt | Aug 2018 - Dec 2018
  • Extract lots of linguistic features, such as part-of-speech tags, stop words, and punctuation marks, from the TOEFL essays written by English Learners whose native language is one of eleven languages.
  • Tune the parameters using GridSearch with 5-fold cross-validation.
  • Visualize the confusion matrix of 11 categories.
