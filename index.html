About关于我

I am an eighth-year Ph.D. student of University of North Texas, supervised by Dr. Eduardo Blanco and Dr. Yan Huang. Before entering UNT, I obtained my master degree in Information Science and bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, from the University of Pittsburgh and Shenzhen University, respectively.

My research interests include natural language processing and deep learning. Specifically, I am currently working on the project of mining spatio-temporal knowledge from social media. Besides the research, I am also interested in working out in gym and travelling.
