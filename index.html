I am a sixth-year Ph.D. student of HiLT lab, College of Engineering, University of North Texas, supervised by Dr. Eduardo Blanco and Dr. Yan Huang. Before entering UNT, I obtained my master degree in Information Science and bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, from the University of Pittsburgh and Shenzhen University, respectively.

Research Experiences

Spatio-Temporal Information Extraction

UNT | Aug 2019 - Feb 2023
  • Propose new tasks of determining if Twitter users are located in the mentioned location and how long they will stay at the geotagged location.
  • Create multiple corpora with quality guaranteed by filtering out bad annotators and unreliable annotations. Design interfaces to collect annotations on Amazon Mechanical Turk.
  • Build multimodal and context-aware neural networks to tackle these problems. Experimental results show that both text and image are essential, and incorporating temporal commonsense is beneficial.
